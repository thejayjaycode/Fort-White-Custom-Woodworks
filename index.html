About Fort White

Founded in 1982, Fort White Custom Woodworks has been providing quality woodworking services and premium lumber to professionals and hobbyists alike. Our team of skilled craftsmen take pride in every project, whether it's custom furniture, architectural millwork, or milling your lumber to precise specifications.

With over 15,000 square feet of workshop and storage space, we maintain an extensive inventory of domestic and exotic hardwoods, along with complete milling capabilities to handle projects of any size.

We're committed to sustainable practices and source our lumber from responsible suppliers who share our respect for the craft and the environment.
